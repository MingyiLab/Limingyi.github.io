I completed my MA in Economics in 2023 from The Wang Yanan Institute for Studies in Economics at Xiamen University and will join the Economics PhD program at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen) this September. My research interests include urban economics, labor economics, and structural estimation, focusing on urban-rural inequality during urbanization.

I have developed interdisciplinary skills in statistics and computer science and am excited to apply this knowledge to economics. I believe these skills will enhance causal inference and counterfactual analysis in my research.
