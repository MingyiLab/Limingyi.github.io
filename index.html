Mingyi Li

I completed my MA in Economics in 2023 from The Wang Yanan Institute for Studies in Economics in Xiamen University and enroll in the Economics PhD program in Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen) this year. My research interests span urban economics, labor economics, and structural estimation, with a particular focus on urban-rural inequality during urbanization.

To achieve my academic endeavors, I have cultivated a inter-disciplinary skill set that encompasses economics, statistics, and computer science. I intend to integrate advanced Machine Learning techniques, such as Causal Forest, XgBoost, CNN and LSTM, into my research, into causal inference and counterfactual analysis. I am excited about the potential these methodologies can contribute to my research and keenly anticipating the commencement of my PhD journey.
